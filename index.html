Personnel Files

Key individuals involved in the Diplomatic Relations operation.

Kunem

Kunem is an emerging voice in contemporary Afrobeats, blending smooth melodies with emotionally charged storytelling and global influences. Known for a sound that bridges West African rhythms with modern R&B and pop sensibilities, Kunem crafts records that feel both intimate and expansive. His music often explores themes of love, ambition, identity, and late-night reflection, carried by a distinctive vocal style and clean, atmospheric production. As his catalog grows, Kunem continues to position himself as a boundary-pushing artist with international appeal.

Jerome da Diplomat

Jerome da Diplomat is a dynamic hip-hop artist known for his sharp lyricism, global perspective, and polished, statesman-like delivery. Blending introspective storytelling with confident, streetwise energy, his music navigates themes of ambition, culture, power, and personal evolution. Drawing influence from both classic rap traditions and international sounds, Jerome da Diplomat crafts records that feel calculated yet authentic—balancing grit with sophistication. With a voice that commands attention and a brand rooted in strategy and presence, he continues to carve out a lane defined by intellect, style, and intention.
